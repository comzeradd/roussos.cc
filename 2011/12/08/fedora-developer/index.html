Being a Fedora Developer (aka Package Maintainer)

2011.12.08 - #opensource #fedora

About a month ago I was sponsored and got accepted as a Fedora Package Maintainer. Being already a Fedora Ambassador I wanted a more technical way to contribute to the project.

Currently I only maintain two packages, but I hope I'll have the time in the near future to help more (I already digging around Sound SIG 's wishlist for new packages). It's great to contribute to such a vivid community project.

Many thanks to Christoph who sponsored me and still helps when needed.
